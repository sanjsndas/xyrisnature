🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is as important to us as the soil beneath our feet. We believe in transparency, care, and respect for every visitor who shares their time with us.

🌱

Information we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide — such as your name and email address when you fill out our contact form, subscribe to our newsletter, or leave a comment. We also collect anonymous usage data via cookies to improve your experience. We never sell or rent your personal data.

🌿 In short:

  • We collect what you give us (name, email, messages).
  • We use cookies to understand how you interact with our site.
  • We never share your data with third parties for marketing.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries — when you reach out via the contact form, we use your email to reply.
  • To improve our content — we analyze anonymous trends to see which articles resonate most with our readers.
  • To send occasional updates — if you opt in, we may share new articles, seasonal gardening tips, or event news. You can unsubscribe anytime.

We will never use your data for purposes other than those clearly stated here. Your trust is the root of our community.

Cookies & tracking

We use minimal, privacy-first cookies to remember your preferences and to gather anonymous analytics. These are strictly necessary for the site’s functionality and to understand how our readers engage with our content. You can manage cookie settings in your browser at any time.

For more details, please see our Cookies Policy.

Third-party services

We occasionally embed content from trusted platforms like YouTube or Vimeo. These services may set their own cookies. We choose providers that prioritize user privacy. We also use a privacy-friendly analytics tool that does not track individual users across the web.

We do not allow third-party advertising networks on our site — no intrusive ads, no data brokers.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify — update or correct your information.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data at any time.
  • Object — opt out of any non-essential processing.

To exercise any of these rights, simply reach out to us — we’ll respond within 48 hours.

Data security

We treat your data with the same care we treat our seedlings. Our website uses encrypted connections (HTTPS), and we regularly review our security practices. We do not store sensitive financial information, as we do not process payments directly on this site.

🔐 Our commitment: We will notify you promptly in the unlikely event of a data breach, and we will always act in the best interest of your privacy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will always post the latest version here, and we encourage you to review it periodically. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via a prominent notice on our homepage or by email.
