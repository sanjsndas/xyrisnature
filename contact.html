❓ Before you write

How soon can I expect a reply?

We reply within 48 hours. If you’re writing about a specific article, we’ll do our best to answer in detail — sometimes it takes a little longer to dig into the soil science!

Do you accept guest contributions?

Yes! We welcome thoughtful, original pieces from growers and researchers. Please send a pitch with your idea — we’ll get back to you within a week.

Can I submit a seed or variety for review?

We love hearing about heirlooms and regional varieties. Drop us a note and we’ll coordinate — we’re always looking for stories behind the seeds.
