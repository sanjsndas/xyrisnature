    ❓ frequently asked questions

    How do I start a no-dig garden on heavy clay?

    Begin with a thick layer of cardboard, then add 15–20 cm of compost and mulch. Over time, earthworms and roots will improve structure. Avoid tilling — let biology do the work.

    What are the best cover crops for nitrogen fixation?

    Crimson clover, vetch, and field peas are excellent winter annuals. For summer, cowpeas and sunn hemp perform well in warm climates. Always inoculate seeds with the right rhizobium strain.

    How can I improve water retention without plastic?

    Add organic matter (compost, leaf mould), use swales and berms, and apply woodchip mulch. Biochar is also a long-term solution — it holds water and nutrients in the root zone.
