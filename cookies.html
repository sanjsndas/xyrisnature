🍪 Cookies Policy

We use only the essential crumbs — no data harvesting, no tracking across the web. Just enough to make your visit feel like home.

🌾

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our content. We treat them like seeds — used sparingly and with care.

We do not use cookies for advertising, profiling, or any form of cross-site tracking. Our approach is minimal, transparent, and privacy-first.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences — such as if you’ve dismissed a notice or selected a theme.
  • Analyze anonymous usage — we count page visits and popular content, but we never track individual users. All data is aggregated.
  • Enable basic functionality — for example, to keep you logged in if you have an account (though we don’t currently offer accounts).

🌱 Our cookie philosophy:

  • No third-party tracking cookies.
  • No advertising or retargeting cookies.
  • No data sold or shared.

Cookies we use

Below is a list of the specific cookies we set and what they do. All are strictly necessary or performance-related.

Name Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preference so we don’t show the banner repeatedly. 1 year
xyris_session Maintains basic session state (e.g., if you’ve interacted with a form). Session
_ga_* (anonymized) Used by our privacy-friendly analytics to count unique visitors and page views. No personal data is collected. 2 years
preferred_view Stores your preferred content layout or reading mode (if available). 30 days

We do not use any third-party cookies for advertising, social media, or cross-site tracking.

Your choices

You are in control. You can:

  • Accept or decline — when you first visit, we ask for your consent. You can change your mind at any time.
  • Manage in your browser — most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.
  • Opt out of analytics — we use a cookieless analytics alternative that respects Do-Not-Track signals. You can also disable JavaScript, though it may affect some interactions.

If you have any questions about our cookie use, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or regulations. We’ll always post the latest version here, and if changes are significant, we’ll notify you via a banner or email.
